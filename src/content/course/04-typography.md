Typography is 90% of web design. 

## Font Selection
We use **Outfit** for headings to give it a modern, techy feel, and **Inter** for body text for maximum readability.

## Hierarchy
- **H1**: Large and bold (3rem+) for impact.
- **Body**: Comfortable size (16px-18px) with generous line height (1.6).

Good typography makes your educational content easier to digest!

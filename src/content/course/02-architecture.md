In this lesson, we dive deep into the **JAMstack** philosophy.

## What is JAMstack?
JAM stands for **JavaScript, APIs, and Markup**. 

Unlike WordPress, which builds your page every time someone visits, JAMstack builds your page **once** when you push your code. This makes your site:
- **Faster**: Serving static files is instantaneous.
- **Safer**: No database to hack.
- **Cheaper**: Hosting static files is often free.

## Our Tech Stack
- **Astro**: The engine that builds our HTML.
- **GitHub**: The "Database" where our content lives.
- **Netlify**: The "Cloud" that serves our site and handles our logins.

# The Future of Jamstack

The web is changing. No longer do we need heavy databases for simple content. With tools like Astro, we can build sites that are fundamentally faster, more secure, and easier to scale.

## Why move away from WordPress?

While WordPress served us well for decades, the overhead of PHP and MySQL can often slow down performance. By pre-rendering our content to static HTML, we ensure that our users get the fastest possible experience.

### Key Benefits:
- **Speed**: Instant load times.
- **Security**: No database to hack.
- **Scalability**: Host anywhere with a CDN.
